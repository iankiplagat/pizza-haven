Milestones


Pizza Haven was founded back in 1980 when the founder and CEO, Ian Kiplagat, moved from the US to Canada. While there, he met Ari, a beautiful, smart, and dynamic young woman, who introduced him to the world of endless possibilities. The project was born out of a joke that Ari made one evening as the two sat at the Riverdale Park East while facing the city skyline, each with a slice of pizza in their hand.

"This pizza tastes awful. I can make better pizza," Ari said disgruntedly. And just like that the idea of the haven we are all proud to associate ourselves with came up. It did not take long before Ian set things in motion and finally the project came to life in one chilly morning in the month of August.

With over 50 branches in 4 major cities, Pizza Haven seeks to expand its operations to over 100 countries around the world by the year 2060.

Currently, Ari is the CEO & Founder of Ari Community, a nonprofit foundation that seeks to feed and educate over 60 million children all over the world.
